A FOR-PROFIT CHILD CARE center may participate if at least 25 percent of the children in care (enrolled or licensed capacity, whichever is lower) receive the child subsidy (Title XX) or meet the household income eligibility guidelines used in the federal school lunch program.
•
FOR-PROFIT ADULT DAY CARE centers may participate in the CACFP if 25 percent of the enrolled participants receive Title XIX and/or Title XX benefits.
•
NON-PROFIT CHILD AND ADULT DAY CARE centers qualify regardless of enrollment.
•
NOTE: Day care homes must participate with a sponsor. They cannot contract directly with TDA.
